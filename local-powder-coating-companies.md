##What Is Powder Coating and Why It Matters
Powder coating is a modern finishing process that involves applying a dry powder to metal surfaces and curing it under heat. This creates a strong, protective layer that resists corrosion, chipping, and fading.
Unlike traditional paint, powder coating provides a thicker and more uniform finish. It is widely used for automotive parts, industrial equipment, metal furniture, and structural components. Businesses benefit from its durability and cost-effectiveness, especially when working with experienced local providers.
